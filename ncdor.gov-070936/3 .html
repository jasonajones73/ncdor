Phase 2 Of Business Recovery Grant Program Launches May 2

May 2, 2022

Phase 2 of the Business Recovery Grant (BRG) program began on Monday, May 2. Many additional businesses now qualify for these grants due to updates to the program’s eligibility criteria. BRG will issue a payment to an eligible North Carolina business that suffered an economic loss of at least 20 percent during the pandemic. The application deadline is Wednesday, June 1.
